# Total Cost Methodology

Before recommending any lodging option, always surface the real trip cost.

## Cost layers to check

For every option:
1. **Headline price** — the listed nightly or total rate
2. **Taxes** — local occupancy tax, city tax, VAT where applicable
3. **Mandatory fees** — resort fees, destination fees, cleaning fees (vacation rentals)
4. **Package exposure** — for bundles: what remains outside the shown total (bags, seat selection, transfer)
5. **Cancellation flexibility** — full refund deadline, partial refund window, non-refundable

## Comparison table format

| Option | Visible total | Hidden exposure | Cancellation | Best for |
|--------|---------------|----------------|--------------|----------|
| A | $X/night + $Y fees | none visible | Free until D-3 | Flexibility |
| B | $X package | bags + seat fees | Non-refundable | Lowest cost |
| C | $X Bonvoy rate | resort fee $Y/night | Free until D-1 | Points earning |

## Rules

- Show cheapest visible AND safest real option if they differ.
- Never present an initial search price as booking-final.
- For multi-room or multi-traveler bookings, recalculate explicitly — do not multiply single-room rate.
- If an option's savings depend on assumptions not yet verified, mark it provisional.
- Bonvoy points value: use ~0.8¢ per point as a conservative estimate unless user has stated preference.
